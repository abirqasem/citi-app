About this app

What will this app do? // Que vamos a hacer?

This app will help you practice the question set provided by UCIS so that you can get it right.

Vamos a ayudar a la gente a practicar las preguntas de UCIS.

The Citizenship Test App tests your knowledge on the common questions asked during the citizenship interview. There are 100 questions. A correct answer adds one point. An incorrect answer subtracts one point. If you don't know the right answer, you can skip the question (no points are added or subtracted). To hear the question and speak the response, choose the audio version. To read and answer the question on the site, choose text. The last option audio and text lets you hear the audio and read the text simultaneously. The official UCIS website for downloading a pdf of the questions is here
